About ITG

The company started as Commerce, Production and Services with Monteiro and Mazuza, both knowledgeable in Instrumentation and Graphic Arts.

With time and the passing of Jorge Mazuza, Carlos Monteiro changed the focus of the company to efforts in the services and the representation of instrument brands

A former employee, Alberto Lopes, electrical/electronic technician and pre-press specialist got partnership in the company taking care of the educational training to costumers, much needed for them to effectively use the instruments supplyed.

Since the year 2000, some consultancy jobs was performed with the "Cat Consultoria" company owned by Ary. The idea of the joining venture was to offer a knowledge backgrond to a comprehensive range of technologies used by the printing insdustries

Finally Carlos Monteiro retired and the two companies joined to continue the services and representation of the Ihara Brand.

The two consultants Ary and Alberto continued the work for the Ihara Brand and the consulting services to the graphic arts industries. Printing Companies using Litho-offset, Rotogravure, Screen printing, Digital (laser, inkjet, proofing systems) started to be served as well as some printing ink manufacturers

Some printer and video monitor callibrations and color profiling was performed as part of the pre-press adjustment programs to the clients. Also in-company training classes was supplyed to the press, pre-press, proofing and quality control departments.

Software for color quality control and automated color formulation was installed, as well as a in-compant course of color measurement fundamentals and the instruments connected to the system and needed for these implementations.

To support the sales of these systems, demonstrations were provided in international trade shows, technical panels were presented at congresses and a bi-monthly course of color measurememnt and basics of color sampling was set.
